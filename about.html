How I Got Here...

My coding journey began in 2020 with a challenging work project. I was tasked with reviewing hundreds of documents containing thousands of names, a process that was repetitive, time-consuming, and prone to human error. While searching for a solution, I discovered that coding could easily automate the task. This sparked my curiosity and ignited my passion for coding.

Years later, I can now create websites from scratch and deploy them to cloud platforms with ease. What started as a solution to a tedious problem has evolved into a rewarding and exciting career in web development.
